Why Choose Truk-It?

Convenient

At Back-it-up, we make moving large items easy and stress-free. Our platform connects you with local truck owners, allowing you to request a delivery from the comfort of your home. Whether it's a new couch, a grill, or storage-bound belongings, our service ensures that your items are transported safely and efficiently. No more struggling to find a truck or dealing with the hassle of traditional delivery services.

Prompt

We understand the importance of timely deliveries. With Back-it-up, you can count on prompt service that fits your schedule. Our network of dedicated truck owners is available 24/7, ready to pick up and deliver your items when you need them. We prioritize quick response times and efficient transportation to ensure your items arrive at their destination without delay.

Affordable

Moving large items shouldn't break the bank. Back-it-up offers competitive pricing that provides excellent value for your delivery needs. By connecting directly with truck owners, we eliminate the middleman, reducing costs and passing the savings on to you. Enjoy affordable rates without compromising on quality or reliability.

How It Works

1. Sign Up

Create an account as a driver or customer to get started. Users who sign up for an account will get a coupon for savings on their first delivery

2. Request Delivery

Fill out a form with your delivery details. We will review and contact you via phone or email regarding next steps.

3. Receive Delivery

We will reach out to you via phone or email to coordinate delivery, and will deliver to your home ASAP
